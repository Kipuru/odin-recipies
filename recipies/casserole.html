Grandma's Ground Beef Casserole

Ever wanted to make a casserole the way grandma can?

Look no further, this is the definitive and complete guide on how to make Grandma's Ground Beef Casserole!

The best part about this recipie is that it's easy to make, but still tastes amazing!

It's also safe to freeze, so you can save it for later!

I recommend having this casserole with a fresh cup of orange juice and a ceaser salad on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 package of pasta (egg noodles are best!)
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 3 ounces of softened cream cheese
  • 1 diced white onion
  • Shredded cheddar cheese (or monterey jack to spice it up!)

Steps

  1. Cook and stir ground beef for 5-7 minutes. Drain and discard grease.
  2. Mix tomato sauce, suga, garlic salt, and salt into the ground beef, simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Cover skillet and move away from heat; let cool to room temperature.
  4. Bring lightly salted water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in boiling water, stirring occasionally for 7-9 minutes. Drain. (best to do this while the ground beef + sauce is simmering)
  5. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F, or 175 degress C. Grease casserole dish. (do while egg noodles are cooking)
  6. In a bowl, mix sour cream, cream cheese, and onion together.
  7. Layer 1/2 egg noodles into casserole dish. Follow with 1/2 sour cream mixture, then 1/2 ground beef mixture. Repeat.
  8. Sprinkle cheddar cheese on top, then bake for 25-30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and golden brown.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
